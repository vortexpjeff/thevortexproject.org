How to read this image

Corrected reflectance combines red, green, and blue channels to approximate daylight human vision. White areas are often cloud, snow, or bright surface. Cloud can hide the ground. Swath edges, seams, and missing areas come from orbital coverage and processing; they are not landscape boundaries. Today's layer refreshes as NASA publishes additional passes.
