    How to read this image

    Corrected reflectance combines red, green, and blue channels to approximate daylight human vision. White areas are often cloud, snow, or bright surface. Cloud can hide the ground. Swath seams and missing areas come from orbital coverage and processing, not landscape boundaries. Today’s layer fills in as NASA publishes additional passes.
